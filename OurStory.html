Why Blink Audio?

At Blink Audio Systems we believe everyone has a musician inside of them. One could list a million reasons why so few people actually get in touch with that side of themselves, but when it comes down to it, people get discouraged by the time sink needed to get anywhere with modern production software.

By cutting out the fat of production software, we aim to drastically shorten the time between an idea and a fully realized piece of music.
