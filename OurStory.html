Why Blink Audio?

At Blink Audio Systems we believe everyone has a musician inside of them. We also believe FAR too many people go through life out of touch with their inner artist. We strive constantly to change that reality by distilling music production software down to its core components allowing any novice musician the chance to create great music in the blink of an eye.


By cutting out the fat of production software, we aim to drastically shorten the time between an idea and a fully realized piece of music.


In order to make as much impact as possible for entry level producers, Blink Audio focuses on developing VST plugins that will decrease the time required to prepare tracks for recording and beat production. This is done through a simplification of graphic interfaces as well as a stramlining of leveling and basic instrument layering.
